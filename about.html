✉️ Founders’ Message

As-salamu alaykum wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh.
Peace, mercy, and blessings of Allah be upon you all.

All praise is due to Allah ﷻ, the Creator and Sustainer of all that exists — the Most Merciful, the Most Compassionate — who entrusted us with the Amanah (trust) of protecting this Earth.

“Indeed, in the creation of the heavens and the earth and the alternation of the night and the day are signs for people of understanding.” — Surah Aal-Imran (3:190)
“And We have not sent you, [O Muhammad], except as a mercy to the worlds.” — Surah Al-Anbiya (21:107)

As the founders of Green Islam Bangladesh, we are committed to reviving Islamic environmental values through education, action, and spiritual awareness.

Our mission is to unite faith and sustainability—empowering Muslims to lead in climate justice, green innovation, and eco-conscious living.

Caring for the Earth is more than a duty — it is an act of worship. From Eco-Masjid initiatives to tree plantations and Green AI, every project we launch is guided by the Qur’an and the Sunnah of our beloved Prophet ﷺ.

Let us rise together — for the sake of Allah — to become true stewards (khulafa) of the Earth: serving humanity, protecting nature, and spreading mercy.

Dr. Mohammad Sahabuddin Shariar Hasan Raisul Maharub

May Allah bless our efforts and guide us all towards righteousness.

Jazakum Allahu khairan (May Allah reward you with goodness).

With peace and blessings
